Safety and Privacy of your data

Providing highest level of safety and privacy to your data is our first priority. We strictly follow the following guidelines to protect your data and personal information:

  • We never share your data with anyone. Our researchbots use pull operations where information relevant to your research are pulled from public databases and pushed to your notification area without sharing your data anywhere.
  • All our servers are encrypted and secure.
  • You can further protect your account by email/phone OTP option. With this option, you need your mobile phone or email to access your Narad/Vexperiment/Labma account.
  • All our Login screens are protected by Google reCAPTCHA .
  • To further protect your data
      Before you begin, make sure the weblogin page (URL) starts with one of the following:
    • https://www.sanmed.ca/..
    • https://narad.sanmed.ca/..
    • https://vexperiment.sanmed.ca/..
    • https://labma.sanmed.ca/...

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Narad suitable for all biologists or only useful for specific branch of biological sciences?

Narad is design keeping the fundamental research pipeline of a biological system in mind. The simple framework of Model System → Protocol → Experiment makes Narad useful to all biologists.

What should I mention in the Model system if I am doing simulatio studies ?

For simulation-based studies, model system represents the type of simulation and/or framework used(e.g., Monte Carlo, Gillespie, Brownian Dynamics, Cellular Potts Model, Chaste etc.).
